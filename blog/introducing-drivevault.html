Introducing DriveVault: Search Every Drive Offline

Published September 22, 2025 • 5 min read

If you work with photography, video, or large creative projects, chances are you have more than one external hard drive sitting on your desk. Over time, those drives multiply. One holds a client project, another is full of raw footage, and a third has backups you hope you’ll never need to use.

The problem? When you need a file, you’re left guessing which drive it’s on — and plugging in drive after drive until you find it. It’s slow, it’s frustrating, and it breaks your flow.

The DriveVault Solution

DriveVault was built to solve exactly this problem. Instead of hunting across drives, you scan each drive once. DriveVault creates a local catalog of everything on it — names, sizes, dates, metadata, even thumbnails — stored securely on your Mac.

  • Search all drives offline — find files instantly without plugging anything in.
  • Browse familiar views — Column, Icon, or List view, just like Finder.
  • Preview files — check EXIF for photos, video details, and more without opening another app.

Built for Creatives, Optimized for Mac

DriveVault is designed for the people who need it most:

  • Filmmakers tracking terabytes of footage
  • Photographers managing years of shoots
  • Agencies and studios keeping projects organized
  • Anyone with multiple drives and no time to waste

It’s built natively for macOS, with smooth navigation and a privacy-first design.

More Than Just Search

  • Organize files with tags, notes, and star ratings.
  • Compare drives to detect duplicates or missing backups.
  • Generate reports to see storage usage and trends.
  • Create folder templates for consistent project structures.
