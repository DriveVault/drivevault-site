How DriveVault Works

A simple, privacy-first workflow designed for working with lots of external drives.

Scan your drives

Connect each drive once and scan it in DriveVault. Choose Full Scan to generate offline image previews and rich metadata, or Quick Scan for near-instant cataloguing of very large drives. Everything happens locally in a fast, native macOS-style interface.

Search everything instantly

Use powerful global search across your entire drive library to find files and folders in seconds. Search by filename, folder name, or full path and DriveVault will surface exactly where the file lives - even when the drive is unplugged.

Use DriveVault's advanced features like comparing backup drives, generating detailed reports, sharing drive libraries with other users, and creating structured folder templates to your drives. DriveVault's powerful features are simple to use, but built for professionals.
